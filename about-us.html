Who We Are

About Triptoniq

The story of Trip Toniq began with a single inspiring conversation — a suggestion that founder Nishant should start his own travel company dedicated to senior travelers. Having led tours across India and abroad with various reputed agencies, Nishant carried years of experience and a deep understanding of what truly makes a journey memorable.

With the dream of offering comfortable, guided, and worry-free travel experiences for seniors, Trip Toniq came to life. Our very first group set off for Rajasthan in December 2021, and within just four months, we proudly served our first 100 travelers. Today, we’ve had the privilege of crafting unforgettable journeys for more than 2,500 happy explorers—and the number continues to grow!

At Trip Toniq, our mission is to create travel experiences that combine comfort, affordability, and meaningful exploration. Every tour we design is guided, well-organized, and stress-free, ensuring you focus only on what matters most—enjoying every moment.

Whether it’s an offbeat destination or a classic getaway, we promise journeys that feel exclusive, safe, and beautifully memorable.
